      Now

      Updated April 2026 · Inspired by Derek Sivers

      What I'm working on

      Thinking seriously about geopolitics and the structures underneath the headlines. Most of what gets reported is the surface. I'm more interested in the architecture beneath.

      What I'm reading

      Kishore Mahbubani on the Asian century. Re-reading Thucydides. And a lot of energy sector reports that I probably should not find as interesting as I do.

      What I'm thinking about

      How Bangladesh fits into a world that is reorganizing itself fast. Whether our institutions are being built for the world of 2035 or the world of 2005.

      Where I am

      Dhaka. Writing here, thinking everywhere.

      I'm Muhaimen Siddiquee. I write about geopolitics, Bangladesh, and the kind of questions that don't have clean answers.

      My interests sit at the intersection of how things work — power, economics, institutions — and why most of us misunderstand them until it's too late.

      By profession I work in brand and communications. By inclination I am a student of history and politics.

      This site is my public notebook.
